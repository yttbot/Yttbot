Ytt - Discord Bot

Ytt is designed with both ease of use and functionality in mind. Our purpose is to be able to provide a platform that allows Discord users to communicate more efficiently, and to let Administrators manage their servers more easily, and better. Look at a list of features by clicking the button below!

Learn more Get it now

Administration

Yttbot comes with many useful administrator features out of the box, including support for muting, clearing messages, and setting customizable welcome and goodbye messages when a user joins or leaves your server!

Guild Features

Out of the box, we include features such as: polling, tags (OMG!), listing online users, total members, showing individual information about users, and total administrators, just to make it easier to get the information you need.

Music and Fun

Because we're gamers too, we included a full-fledged music player that can play music from youtube and soundcloud, along with fun commands like /meme and /fml - try them out! We're working on camera features!
